Sport Assistance

Committed to providing users with fitness services and guidance.

Introduction of Sport Assitance

Sport Assistance is a project that provides services, guidance and sports assistance for fitness enthusiasts.

Through this website, users can search for outdoor sports venues near them, filter the venues according to their needs, and finally determine the most suitable sports venue location.

The website also provides medical facilities near sports venues. If users are injured during exercise, they can get help as soon as possible. What's more,When users feel thirsty when they are out for exercise, we will provide them with the location of a nearby drinking fountain. At the same time, the location of the nearest public toilet will also be displayed in our application.

Our Purpose

As the epidemic subsides in 2021, people's demand for outdoor sports is gradually awakening. In order to encourage everyone to go out for sports, Sport Assitance will be committed to providing various services for all sports enthusiasts.

As a goal, we hope that users will be able to open our website and get the information they want quickly when they need to find a sports location, need emergency services or make an appointment in a minute or two.
